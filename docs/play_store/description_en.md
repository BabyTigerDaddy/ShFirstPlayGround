# Play Store Description (English)

## Short description (80 chars)

Metacognitive questions for the show you just watched with your kid.

## Long description (4,000 chars)

**Ever finished watching a show with your kid and felt stuck on what to ask?**

Developmental research shows that a single **short conversation** after watching content has a stronger effect on a 5-year-old's cognitive and emotional development than the content quality itself. It's called **joint media engagement**, and the parents who do it consistently make a real difference.

The hard part is: *what do I ask?*

**Talk With Kid** answers that question for you. Input the show your child just watched, and the app generates **3 thoughtfully crafted, developmentally appropriate questions** for the moment.

## How it works

1. Type the title of what you watched (e.g., Bluey, Peppa Pig, Numberblocks)
2. Get 3 metacognitive questions tailored to the show
3. Ask your child one or two after the show ends

## What kind of questions

Five categories chosen by developmental balance for 5-year-olds:

- **Emotion** — "Which character looked happiest today, do you think?"
- **Cognition** — "Why do you think that happened? Could it have ended differently?"
- **Recall** — "What's one thing you remember most from today's show?"
- **Application** — "Has something like that happened to you? What did you do?"
- **Comparison** — "Is this character a little like you in any way?"

## Content library

Curated 5-year-old favorites spanning Korean IP and global hits.
